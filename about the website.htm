About this website

  1. The goal of the portal is to help people to manage the waste generated by them at their respective places
  2. The firm, after collecting the garbage, assesses the waste produced at each household and displays the monthly report on the respective profiles of users.
  3. After assessing the waste generated by an individual, depending upon the amount of biodegradable quantity in it, he/she earns credits.


Tips

  1. Reduce or eliminate the use of paper plates and cups.

  2. Store leftover foods in reusable containers instead of single use plastic bags or Polystyrene foam containers.

  3. Buy food that has less packaging

  4. Donate items when possible.
